The multimedia center of Essora Linux

Version 2.2-1 GTK3 MPV Integration YouTube Download IPTV

Oply is the integrated multimedia suite developed specifically for Essora Linux. Instead of separating music playback, IPTV, Internet radio, YouTube downloads and video playback into multiple unrelated applications, Oply combines everything inside a single lightweight interface built around the Essora ecosystem.

Audio Player

Oply includes a complete audio player designed for everyday music playback with playlist support and a clean GTK3 interface integrated into Essora Desktop.

  • Playlist support
  • Modern GTK3 interface
  • Lightweight resource usage
  • Integrated multimedia workflow

Oply Convert — YouTube Downloader

Oply Convert allows users to download both audio and video directly from YouTube without relying on external frontends.

  • YouTube audio download
  • YouTube video download
  • Audio conversion support
  • Integrated download management

Oply Radio

Oply Radio provides access to online radio stations from around the world through a simple integrated interface.

  • Worldwide online radio stations
  • Integrated playback
  • Fast station switching

Oply Video Player

The video player uses MPV integration to provide lightweight and high-performance video playback while maintaining the Essora visual style.

  • MPV backend integration
  • Fullscreen support
  • Integrated controls
  • Modern GTK3 interface

Built-in IPTV Support

Oply also supports IPTV playback. Users can load IPTV playlists directly into the player, and Essora includes a default IPTV list ready to use.

  • IPTV playlist support
  • Integrated IPTV browser
  • Default IPTV list included

Why Oply exists

Oply was created because Essora wanted a multimedia solution fully integrated with its own desktop and ecosystem. Instead of depending on multiple unrelated applications, Oply centralizes audio, video, IPTV, radio and YouTube downloading into a single environment.

Essora Integration

  • Integrated into Essora Desktop
  • Works alongside EssoraFM and Essora Store
  • Designed for Devuan + OpenRC systems
  • Lightweight multimedia environment
