A system built for performance and simplicity

Essora Linux is a distribution focused on being lightweight and practical, ideal for modest hardware and for those who enjoy customization. Based on Devuan, without systemd.

Philosophy

  • Fast boot and low resource usage.
  • Tools ready for daily use.
  • Friendly remastering and customization.

Approach

  • Lightweight edition (Openbox / JWM / Fluxbox).
  • Full edition (depending on release).
  • Community and support via Telegram.
